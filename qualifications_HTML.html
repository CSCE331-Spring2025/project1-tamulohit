Verilog & SystemC

Verilog is an essential skill to know for hardware design. It is one of the most used hardware description languages in industry, and a key tool for front end behavioral development. I would classify myself as an “advanced beginner” in this HDL because I have 3 classes worth of experience using this tool. In ECEN 468, I learnt some advanced capabilities and its limitations in regards to simulation vs. synthesis. SystemC is a newer higher level language designed for quick development and high level synthesis of focused systems (such as ASICs). I was exposed to SystemC in ECEN 468 and have modeled the Canny Edge Detector ASIC detailed in my Projects section in this language as well as Verilog.
